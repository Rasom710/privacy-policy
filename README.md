# Privacy Policy for House of Hypnos

**Last updated: February 2, 2026**

## Overview
House of Hypnos ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our fragrance curation app.

## Information We Collect
When you use our app to receive personalized fragrance recommendations, we collect the following information:

- **Name** - To personalize your experience and correspondence
- **Email address** - To send you fragrance recommendations and updates
- **Phone number** - To contact you regarding your fragrance selections
- **Shipping address** - To deliver fragrances you choose to purchase
- **Fragrance preferences** - Information you provide to help us curate the perfect scent for you

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

- Curate personalized fragrance recommendations tailored to your preferences
- Contact you about fragrance options and purchases
- Process and fulfill your fragrance orders
- Respond to your questions and provide customer support
- Improve our curation services and app experience

## How We Share Your Information
We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your information is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Internal Use Only** - Your submissions are sent directly to our business email for personalized curation services
- **Service Providers** - We use Mailgun (a trusted email delivery service) to transmit your information securely to us. Mailgun's privacy policy can be found at https://www.mailgun.com/legal/privacy-policy/

We do not share your information with any other third parties unless required by law.

## Data Storage and Security
Your information is transmitted securely via Mailgun and stored in our business email account. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention
We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide you with fragrance curation services
- Fulfill any purchases you make
- Comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

## Your Privacy Rights
You have the right to:

- **Access** - Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
- **Correction** - Request correction of any inaccurate information
- **Deletion** - Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-out** - Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hohcurations@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

## Updates to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Third-Party Links
Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## International Users
If you are accessing our app from outside the United States, please note that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers and business operations are located.
